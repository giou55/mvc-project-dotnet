An entirely new feature in .NET 7 is the support for tar archives.

---------------------------------------------------------------------------

Also System.Linq got improving adjustments.
The new methods Order and OrderDescending sort an IEnumerable based on the type T. 
At the same time, IQueryable also supports these interfaces.

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

The DateTime, DateTimeOffset, TimeSpan, and Time-Only classes can now be used with microseconds and nanoseconds.

---------------------------------------------------------------------------------

AOT stands for Ahead of Time, and the Native AOT project has been moved from experimental to official.

----------------------------------------------------------------------------------

.NET 7 will include full annotations for all assemblies and types in the Microsoft.Extensions.Configuration namespace.

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

.NET 7 includes improvements to System.Text.Json serialization.
